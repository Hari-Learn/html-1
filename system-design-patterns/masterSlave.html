Master-Slave Architecture (ELI10)

🧠 What is it?

One main computer (Master) controls many helper computers (Slaves).

❓ Why use it?

To handle lots of work without slowing down.

The master gives instructions, helpers do the work.

⚙️ How does it work?

  • 👑 Master → Handles writes and decisions
  • 👷 Slaves → Handle reads and support tasks
🧸 Think of it like:
👩‍🏫 Teacher (Master)
👨‍🎓 Students (Slaves helping with tasks)
