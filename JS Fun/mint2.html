Discover Linux Mint

What is Linux Mint?

Linux Mint is a modern, free, and open-source desktop operating system based on Ubuntu and Debian. It emphasizes ease of use, performance, and a comfortable user interface for new and experienced users alike.

Why Choose Linux Mint?

Desktop Versions

Linux Mint offers Cinnamon (modern & full-featured), MATE (classic & light), and Xfce (ultra-light). Choose the one that suits your hardware and workflow best.

More About Linux Mint

Linux Mint is known for its stability and long-term support. It avoids radical design changes, providing a consistent experience over time. Mint updates are managed via a custom Update Manager, giving users control over when and how to update their system safely.

It also includes Timeshift, a powerful snapshot tool, that lets you roll back your system if something goes wrong.
