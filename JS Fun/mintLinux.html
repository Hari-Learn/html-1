What is Linux Mint?

Linux Mint is a free and open-source Linux distribution based on Ubuntu and Debian. It's designed to be easy to use, stable, and highly customizable. Mint is particularly popular among users switching from Windows, thanks to its familiar interface and comprehensive software out-of-the-box.
