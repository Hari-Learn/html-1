What is Maltego?

Explore digital connections with a powerful visual tool.

Introduction

Maltego is a tool that helps you see how things on the internet are connected. It’s like a mapmaker, but instead of showing roads, it shows links between websites, people, emails, and more.

How Does It Work?

You start with one piece of information—like an email address or website—and Maltego finds related things. For example, from a website, it might find other sites on the same server, or the person who registered it.

What Can It Show?

  • Who owns a domain name
  • What servers a website uses
  • Linked email addresses or social profiles
  • Other websites connected to the same person

Why Use Maltego?

It helps people like security experts, journalists, and investigators find patterns and connections that are hard to see just by looking at raw data. The tool makes it easy to build a picture from scattered pieces.

How Does It Look?

Maltego displays information in a graph—dots for pieces of data, and lines that show how they're connected. It’s like seeing a spider web of information that tells a story.
