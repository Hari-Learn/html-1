🚀 AI Deployment Checklist

🧠 Model Validation

Before using your AI, make sure it's actually smart enough. Test it with real examples to see if it gives correct answers.

📊 Data Integrity

Your AI learns from data. If the data is messy or wrong, your AI will also be wrong. Keep your data clean and accurate.

🔌 API Layers

APIs are like bridges that let apps talk to your AI. Build simple and safe ways for others to use your model.

🔐 Security Controls

Protect your AI from bad users. Add passwords, limits, and checks so no one can misuse it.

🏗 Infrastructure Setup

This is where your AI lives (servers/cloud). Make sure it’s fast, stable, and can handle many users.

📡 Monitoring Systems

Keep an eye on your AI. Check if it's working, slow, or making mistakes.

🔍 Observability

Understand what’s happening inside your AI. Logs and metrics help you debug problems easily.

🧾 Versioning

Save different versions of your AI. If something breaks, you can go back to an older one.

📝 Testing & Documentation

Test everything before launch. Write simple guides so others know how to use and maintain your AI.
