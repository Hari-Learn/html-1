Core AI Concepts

Model Context (MC)

Model Context refers to all the information provided to an AI model during inference, including prompts, system instructions, and conversation history.

Embeddings

Embeddings convert text into numerical vectors, enabling similarity search, clustering, and deeper semantic understanding of content.

Model Routing

Model routing determines the best AI model for a task by balancing cost, latency, and complexity—ensuring efficient and optimal performance.
