Advanced AI Techniques

🧪

Evals

Think of evals like **tests for AI**. Just like exams check how well you learned something, evals check how well an AI model performs.

They help answer: “Is the AI giving correct answers?” and “Can we trust it?”

📚

RAG

RAG is like **an open-book exam for AI**. Instead of only using memory, the AI first looks up helpful information from outside sources.

Then it uses that info to give better, more accurate answers.

🤖🤝🤖

Multi-Agent Systems

Imagine a **team project**, where each person has a role. That’s how multi-agent systems work.

One AI might research, another writes, and another checks errors—working together to solve bigger problems.
