Why EIGRP Is Powerful

  • Uses bandwidth and delay instead of hop count
  • Keeps instant backup routes (DUAL)
  • Very fast convergence
  • Low update overhead

Why RIP Is Avoided

  • Slow
  • 15-hop limit
  • Outdated

Why CCNA Avoids EIGRP

  • Cisco-only focus
  • Not vendor-neutral
  • OSPF preferred
