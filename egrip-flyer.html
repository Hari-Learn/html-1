Why EIGRP Feels “Smart”

  • Looks at bandwidth and delay — not just hop count
  • Keeps backup routes ready (DUAL algorithm)
  • Very fast convergence when links fail
  • Minimal network traffic for updates

Why RIP is Avoided

  • Slow convergence
  • Max 15 hops
  • Outdated logic

Why CCNA Avoids EIGRP

  • Cisco-centric
  • Not fully vendor-neutral
  • OSPF preferred
