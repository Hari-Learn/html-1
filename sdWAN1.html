Core Concepts

Control & Data Plane

SD-WAN separates the control plane (decision-making) from the data plane (traffic forwarding) for centralized and scalable management.

Overlay vs Underlay

Underlay is the physical network (Internet, MPLS, LTE). Overlay is the secure virtual network built on top for intelligent routing.

Cisco Components

vManage (management), vBond (authentication), and vSmart (control plane) work together to automate WAN.

Why Replace MPLS?

Lower cost, better cloud access, centralized automation, improved redundancy, and flexible transport options.

The Future of WAN

Many students ignore SD-WAN, but automation-based WAN is growing fast. Enterprises are rapidly shifting from traditional MPLS to intelligent, software-defined architectures.
