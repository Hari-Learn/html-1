Blackboard Design Pattern

Multiple independent components collaborate by reading and writing information to a shared "blackboard".

Shared Blackboard

How It Works

  • Experts analyze available data.
  • They write findings to the blackboard.
  • Other experts read and improve the solution.
  • The process continues until a final result is reached.
Example: Speech recognition systems, AI problem solving, and complex diagnostic systems.
