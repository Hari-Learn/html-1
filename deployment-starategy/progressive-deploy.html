Progressive Delivery

Imagine you made a new game update, but you're not sure if everyone will like it.

So instead of giving it to everyone at once, you give it slowly to a few players first.

How it works:

  • Start with a small group of users
  • Watch if everything works fine
  • Slowly give it to more users

If something goes wrong, you can stop early before everyone is affected.

Simple example:

Like tasting food with a few people before serving it to the whole class.
