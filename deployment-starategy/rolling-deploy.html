🚀 Rolling Deployment

Imagine you have 10 light bulbs 💡 in a room. Instead of changing all at once, you replace them one by one so the room never goes dark.

👉 What is it?

Updating your app step-by-step instead of all at once.

🤔 Why use this?

  • No downtime — app stays live
  • If something breaks, only a small part is affected
  • Safer than updating everything at once

📅 When to use it?

  • When your app must always stay online
  • When you have multiple servers
  • When updates are small and safe
⚠️ Not great if your update requires everything to change at once.
