Rolling Deployment

Update your application smoothly without downtime 🚀

💡 Simple Analogy

Imagine you have 10 light bulbs in a room. Instead of replacing all at once, you swap them one by one so the room never goes dark.

What is it?

Updating your app step-by-step instead of all at once.

Why use it?

  • ✅ No downtime
  • ✅ Smaller impact if failure occurs
  • ✅ Safer updates

When to use?

  • 📌 Always-on applications
  • 📌 Multiple servers
  • 📌 Small, safe updates

Not ideal if your update requires everything to change at once.
