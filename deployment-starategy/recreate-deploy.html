Recreate Deployment

Replace everything at once — simple but causes downtime 🔁

🏪 Simple Analogy

Imagine closing your shop, updating everything inside, and reopening later. During that time, no customers can enter.

What is it?

You stop the old app completely and then start the new one.

Why use it?

  • ✅ Simple to implement
  • ✅ No version conflicts
  • ✅ Works well for major changes

When to use?

  • 📌 Downtime is acceptable
  • 📌 Large system changes
  • 📌 Compatibility issues exist

Users cannot access the app during deployment (downtime required).
