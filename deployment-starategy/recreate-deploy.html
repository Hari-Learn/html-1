🔁 Recreate Deployment

Imagine closing your shop 🏪, changing everything inside, and then reopening. During that time, customers can’t enter.

👉 What is it?

You stop the old app completely and then start the new one.

🤔 Why use this?

  • Simple and easy to understand
  • No confusion between old and new versions
  • Good when big changes are needed

📅 When to use it?

  • When downtime is okay
  • When system changes are large
  • When compatibility issues exist
⚠️ Downside: Users cannot access the app during deployment.
