VPN (Virtual Private Network)

What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a technology that creates a secure and encrypted connection between your device and the internet. It protects your data and hides your real IP address.

What Problem Does a VPN Solve?

When you use the internet without protection, your data can be seen by ISPs, hackers, or attackers on public Wi-Fi networks. VPNs prevent data interception and tracking.

Why Do We Need a VPN?

  • Protect data on public Wi-Fi
  • Hide real IP address
  • Encrypt internet traffic
  • Improve online privacy

What is a VPN Tunnel?

A VPN tunnel is a secure, encrypted path through the internet. All data sent through this tunnel is protected from outsiders. Only the user and the VPN server can access the data.

Tunnel Creation Commands (Examples)

SSH Tunnel:

ssh -D 1080 user@server_ip

OpenVPN:

sudo openvpn --config client.ovpn
