What is EtherChannel?

Imagine you have one road between two cities. If too many cars use it, traffic happens.

EtherChannel is like combining many roads into one big super road. It connects multiple cables between switches and makes them act like one powerful connection.

When Do We Need It?

We use EtherChannel when:

  • Lots of data is moving between switches
  • Servers need high speed connections
  • We want network reliability

Big companies and data centers use it to keep networks fast and stable.
