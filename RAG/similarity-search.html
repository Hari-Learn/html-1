WHAT IS IT?

Similarity Search

Imagine you have a giant library with millions of books.

Instead of searching by exact words, AI searches by meaning.

Similarity Search helps AI find pieces of information that are most similar to your question.

Question:

"Why is the sky blue?"

⬇️

AI Searches:

Finds documents about light, atmosphere, and colors.

⬇️

Best Match:

AI picks the most relevant information.

WHY DO WE NEED IT?

Why Similarity Search is Important

⚡

Faster Answers

AI does not read everything every time. It quickly finds only the useful parts.

🧠

Smarter Understanding

Even if words are different, AI can still understand similar meanings.

📚

Better Knowledge

RAG uses outside documents, so answers become more accurate and updated.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

How Similarity Search Works in RAG

1

Convert Text into Numbers

AI turns words into special number patterns called embeddings.

2

Compare Meanings

AI compares your question embedding with document embeddings to find the closest meaning.

3

Send Best Results to AI

RAG gives the best matching information to the AI model, so it can generate a helpful answer.
