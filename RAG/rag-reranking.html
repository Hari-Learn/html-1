WHAT IS RERANKING?

What is ReRanking?

Imagine you search:

“How do airplanes fly?”

The AI search system finds many documents.

But some results are more useful than others.

ReRanking means:

  • ✅ Move the best results to the top
  • ✅ Push weaker results lower

Before vs After ReRanking

❌ Before

Random airplane facts
Airport history
Airplane wings explanation

✅ After

Airplane wings explanation
Lift and airflow
How engines help flight
WHY IS IT IMPORTANT?

Why RAG Uses ReRanking

🎯

Better Accuracy

AI sees the most relevant information first, so answers become more correct.

⚡

Faster Understanding

AI spends more attention on important documents instead of weak matches.

🧠

Smarter Responses

Better document order means better context for the AI model.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

How ReRanking Works

1

Search for Documents

The retriever finds many possible matching documents.

2

Check Quality Again

A reranker model carefully compares the question with each document.

3

Reorder the Results

The best documents move to the top of the list.

4

Generate Better Answer

AI now uses the highest-quality context to create the final response.
