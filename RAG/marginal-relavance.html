WHAT IS MMR?

What is Maximal Marginal Relevance?

Imagine you ask AI:

“Tell me about cats.”

AI finds many documents.

But sometimes all top results say almost the same thing.

MMR helps AI choose results that are:

  • ✅ Relevant to the question
  • ✅ Different from each other

Without MMR vs With MMR

❌ Without MMR

Cats are cute pets.
Cats are friendly pets.
Cats are domestic pets.

✅ With MMR

Cat behavior
Cat food
Cat history

HOW DOES IT WORK?

How MMR Works in RAG

1

Find Relevant Documents

AI first searches for documents related to the question.

2

Remove Repetitive Results

MMR checks if documents are too similar to each other.

3

Keep Diverse Useful Information

AI keeps documents that are useful AND different.

4

Generate Better Final Answer

The AI now has richer information to create a smarter response.
