Multimodal RAG

Imagine an AI that can understand not only words, but also pictures, audio, videos, and documents. That is called Multimodal RAG.

What does "Multimodal" mean?

"Multi" means many, and "Modal" means types of information.

📝

Text

🖼️

Images

🎤

Audio

🎥

Video

So what is Multimodal RAG?

Normal RAG mostly works with text documents.

But Multimodal RAG can search and understand:

📄

PDF files and notes

🖼️

Pictures and diagrams

🎧

Voice recordings and audio

🎬

Videos and screen recordings

How does it work?

🔍

1. Search

AI searches text, images, and media.

🧠

2. Understand

AI understands different content types.

🔗

3. Combine

AI combines all useful information.

✅

4. Answer

AI gives a smarter response.

Why is it important?

  • 📚

    Helps AI learn from more than just text.

  • 🖼️

    AI can understand images and diagrams.

  • 🎤

    AI can work with audio and speech.

  • ⚡

    Makes AI assistants more powerful and useful.

Easy Example

You upload:

📄 A science PDF

Multimodal RAG can understand all of them together and answer your questions better.
