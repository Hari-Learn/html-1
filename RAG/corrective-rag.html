Corrective RAG (CRAG)

Imagine you ask an AI a question, and the AI goes to a big library to find the answer. Sometimes it picks the wrong books or bad information. Corrective RAG (CRAG) is like having a smart teacher who checks if the AI found good information before giving you the answer.

First, what is RAG?

RAG stands for:

Retrieval + Augmented + Generation

Simple meaning:

  • Retrieval: AI searches for information.
  • Augmented: AI adds that information into its thinking.
  • Generation: AI creates the final answer.

So what is Corrective RAG?

CRAG adds one extra superpower:

"Check if the found information is actually good."
🔍

1. Search

AI searches for information.

🧠

2. Check

AI checks if the information looks useful.

❌

3. Fix

If the info is bad, AI searches again.

✅

4. Answer

AI gives a better final answer.

Why is CRAG important?

  • 🎯

    Helps AI give more accurate answers.

  • 🛡️

    Reduces wrong or fake information.

  • 📚

    Makes AI smarter when using documents and databases.

  • ⚡

    Improves trust in AI apps like chatbots and search assistants.

Easy Example

You ask:

"Who won the football world cup in 2018?"

Normal RAG might accidentally pick wrong information.

But CRAG checks the result first and fixes mistakes before answering:

"France won the 2018 FIFA World Cup."

About CRAG

Corrective RAG helps AI systems verify information before answering, making responses smarter, safer, and more accurate.

Quick Benefits

  • ✅ Better AI answers
  • ✅ Fewer mistakes
  • ✅ Smarter document search
  • ✅ More trusted AI systems
