Geoproximity Routing Policy

What is Geoproximity Routing?

Geoproximity routing directs users to cloud resources based on the geographic distance between users and resources. It can also shift traffic using a bias value to expand or reduce traffic to specific regions.

Regional Routing

Traffic Bias Control

Geoproximity routing allows administrators to apply bias values to increase or decrease traffic flow toward specific regions.

Positive Bias (+)

Expands the geographic area served by a resource, attracting more traffic.

More users routed to this region

Negative Bias (-)

Reduces the geographic influence of a resource, decreasing traffic flow.

Fewer users routed to this region
