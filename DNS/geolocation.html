Geolocation-Based Routing Policy

What is Geolocation Routing?

Geolocation-based routing directs users to different cloud resources based on their geographic location such as country, continent, or region.

How Geolocation Routing Works

🌎

North America

Users from the USA and Canada are routed to US servers.

US Region
🌍

Europe

European users connect to nearby European cloud regions.

EU Region
🌏

Asia-Pacific

Asian users are directed to Asia-Pacific infrastructure.

APAC Region

Benefits of Geolocation Routing

⚡

Better Performance

Users access servers closer to their physical location.

🌐

Localized Experience

Deliver region-specific applications and content.

📜

Regulatory Compliance

Keep user data inside required geographic regions.

📈

Scalability

Efficiently distribute global application traffic.

Common Use Cases

Content Localization

Display different languages, currencies, or regional content based on user location.

Multi-Region Applications

Route users to regional data centers for lower response time.

Legal Requirements

Ensure compliance with country-specific data regulations.

Streaming Services

Provide geographically optimized media delivery networks.
