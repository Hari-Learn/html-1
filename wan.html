WAN & Internet Concepts

Learn how the internet works, how global networks are connected, and the role of ISPs, submarine cables, APNIC, IANA, and edge devices.

🌐

WAN (Wide Area Network)

A WAN connects multiple LANs across large geographic areas using technologies like fiber optics, satellites, and leased lines.

🚀

How the Internet Works

The internet is a global network of interconnected systems where data travels in packets through routers, switches, and submarine cables.

📡

APNIC

APNIC manages IP addresses and AS numbers for the Asia-Pacific region, supporting ISPs and organizations.

🏢

ISP (Internet Service Provider)

ISPs connect homes and businesses to the internet using fiber, DSL, wireless, and mobile technologies.

🌊

Submarine Cable Routes

  • SEA-ME-WE 3 & 4
  • IMEWE
  • Bay of Bengal Gateway (BBG)

Mumbai Chennai Kochi Thoothukudi
🗺️

RIR (Regional Internet Registry)

RIRs distribute IP addresses regionally. Examples include APNIC, RIPE NCC, and ARIN.

🔐

IANA

IANA coordinates global IP allocation, domain names, and protocol numbers across the internet.

💻

Edge Devices

Routers, switches, modems, and IoT devices connect users to the network and manage local traffic.
