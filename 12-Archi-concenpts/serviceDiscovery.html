🔍 Service Discovery

Explained like you're 10!

What is Service Discovery?

Imagine you're in a big school and want to find your friend. Instead of remembering their classroom, you ask the school office. 📍 The office tells you where your friend is right now.

👉 In the same way, computers use Service Discovery to find each other without remembering fixed addresses.

Types

👦 Client-Side

The app itself finds where to go. Like calling your friend directly.

🏢 Server-Side

A helper (like a receptionist) sends your request. You don’t need to know where your friend is.

Popular Tools

Netflix tool for finding services
