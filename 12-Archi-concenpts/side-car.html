Sidecar Pattern

📌 What is it?

The Sidecar Pattern attaches auxiliary services (logging, monitoring, security) alongside a main microservice without modifying it.

🚀 Why use it?

  • Separation of concerns
  • Reusable helpers
  • Easier maintenance

⚠ Challenges

  • More resource usage
  • Deployment complexity
  • Extra communication layer

🧒 Explain Like I'm 10

Think of a bike 🚲 with a sidecar:

  • Bike = main service
  • Sidecar = extra helper
  • It adds features without changing the bike
