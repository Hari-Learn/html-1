Consistent Hashing (ELI10)

Simple, smart data distribution 😄

What is Consistent Hashing?

Imagine putting toys 🧸 into boxes 📦. If you add/remove a box, you don’t want to move ALL toys again 😩

Consistent hashing helps move only a few toys instead of everything 👍

How It Works

  • Everything is placed on a big circle 🎡
  • Servers are placed on the circle
  • Data is also placed on the circle
  • Each data goes to the next server clockwise ➡️

Simple Example

Think of a clock 🕒

Servers at 1, 5, 9
Data at 3 → goes to 5
Data at 8 → goes to 9

Why It's Useful

Easy to Grow

Add servers easily 🌱

Less Moving

Only few items move 🚚

Balanced

Spread evenly ⚖️

Cool Trick

Engineers add "virtual nodes" (extra fake servers) to make distribution smoother and fair 😎
