Communication Patterns

Explained like you're 10 👇

Think of apps like friends chatting with each other 📱 — they just use different ways to send messages!

⏳

Synchronous

Like a phone call 📞 — you ask something and wait immediately for the answer.

REST APIs • gRPC
📬

Asynchronous

Like sending a message 💬 — you continue your work and get a reply later.

Queues • Events • Pub/Sub
🚪

Gateway & BFF

Like a receptionist 🧑‍💼 — you talk to one place and it handles everything behind the scenes.

API Gateway • Backend-for-Frontend
💡

Pro Tip

The best systems mix both styles — use fast direct calls when you need instant answers, and messaging when you want flexibility and scale 🚀
