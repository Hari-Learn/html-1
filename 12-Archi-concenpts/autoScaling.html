Auto Scaling

Automatically adjusting resources based on demand

What is Auto Scaling?

Auto scaling automatically increases or decreases the number of servers or computing resources based on traffic or system load.

Types of Scaling

Vertical Scaling

Increase CPU, RAM, or storage of an existing server.

Horizontal Scaling

Add more servers to distribute the load.

Advantages

  • Cost optimization
  • Better performance during traffic spikes
  • Improved system reliability
  • Automatic resource management
