⚡ Auto Scaling

Auto scaling automatically increases or decreases computing resources based on system load or traffic, ensuring optimal performance and cost efficiency.

⚙️ How It Works

  • System monitors traffic/load
  • High demand → adds more servers
  • Low demand → removes extra servers
  • Maintains performance automatically

📦 Types of Scaling

  • Vertical Scaling: Increase CPU, RAM of one server
  • Horizontal Scaling: Add/remove multiple servers

🚀 Advantages

  • Cost optimization 💰
  • Handles traffic spikes 📈
  • Improves reliability 🔒
  • Automatic resource management 🤖
