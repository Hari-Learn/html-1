⚡ Circuit Breaker Pattern

The Circuit Breaker pattern prevents systems from repeatedly calling a failing service. It stops requests temporarily, allowing the system to recover and avoid cascading failures.

🔌 What is Circuit Breaker?

Similar to an electrical circuit breaker, this pattern cuts off requests when failures are detected, preventing damage to the system and improving reliability.

⚙️ How It Works

  • Closed: Requests flow normally
  • Open: Requests are blocked after failures
  • Half-Open: Limited requests to test recovery

🚀 Benefits

  • Prevents cascading failures
  • Improves system stability
  • Gives failing services time to recover
  • Enhances fault tolerance
