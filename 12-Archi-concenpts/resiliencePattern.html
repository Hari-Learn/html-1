🛡️ Resilience Patterns in Microservices

🤔 What is this?

Imagine your system is like a group of friends working together. If one friend gets tired or slow, others should not panic or crash. These patterns help your system stay calm and keep working.

⏱ Timeouts

Don’t wait forever! If one service is too slow, stop waiting after some time and move on.

🔁 Retries

If something fails, try again a few times. Sometimes problems are temporary.

🚦 Rate Limits

Don’t allow too many requests at once. This keeps your system from getting overwhelmed.

⚡ Circuit Breaker

If a service keeps failing, stop calling it for a while. Give it time to recover.

🛑 Overload Protection

Slow down or reject extra work when your system is too busy. This prevents everything from crashing.

🌊 Cascading Failures

One failure should NOT break everything. Keep problems contained so they don’t spread.

💡 Why It Matters

These patterns help your system degrade gracefully. That means even if something goes wrong, your app still works a little instead of completely collapsing.
