Decompose by Subdomain (DDD)

A simple way to organize complex systems

🧠 ELI10 Explanation

Imagine a school with different sections:

📚

Library

🏀

Sports

🏫

Classrooms

Each section works independently with its own rules and responsibilities.

In software, we call these sections subdomains.

Each subdomain handles its own logic, making the system easier to understand, build, and maintain.

✨ Why it matters

  • ✅ Clear separation of responsibilities
  • ✅ Easier to scale and modify
  • ✅ Teams can work independently
  • ✅ Reduces complexity
