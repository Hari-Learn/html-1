Decompose by Subdomain (DDD)

ELI10 Explanation

Think of a school. There are different sections like:

  • Library 📚
  • Sports 🏀
  • Classrooms 🏫

Each section has its own rules and work.

In software, we call these sections "subdomains". Each subdomain handles its own logic without mixing things up.

This helps keep things clean and easy to manage.
