Business Capability Explained

🧠 ELI10 Explanation

Imagine a big toy store 🧸. If one person tries to do everything, it becomes messy and slow.

💳 Payments

This part handles money — like paying at the counter.

📦 Orders

This part keeps track of what people buy and prepares it.

👤 Users

This part remembers customers and their details.

👉 Big idea: Break one big system into small parts, so each part does one job really well!
