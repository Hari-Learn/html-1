Decompose by Business Capability

ELI10 Explanation

Imagine a big toy store. Instead of one person doing everything, you have different people for billing, packing, and selling toys.

In software, we do the same thing. We split the system into parts like:

  • Payments service 💳
  • Orders service 📦
  • User service 👤

Each service does one job really well!
