Service Mesh (Istio)

What is it?

A service mesh manages how different parts of an app talk to each other. It handles security, traffic, and monitoring.

ELI10 Explanation

Imagine a traffic police 🚦 controlling cars so they don’t crash, go the right way, and stay safe. That’s what a service mesh does for apps!

Example

If one service is slow, the mesh redirects traffic to a faster one automatically.
