Decomposition Patterns

Decomposition patterns help break down a monolithic system into smaller, manageable microservices. Choosing the right strategy improves scalability, maintainability, and team productivity.

Microservices Architecture System Design

Business Capability

Break your system based on core business functions such as Orders, Payments, and Users. Each service represents a real-world business capability.

Subdomain (DDD)

Based on Domain-Driven Design, divide the system into bounded contexts where each subdomain has its own logic and data model.

Why Decomposition Matters

  • Improves scalability by isolating services
  • Enables independent deployments
  • Reduces complexity in large systems
  • Aligns teams with business domains
