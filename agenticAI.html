Agentic AI

AI systems capable of autonomous decision-making and multi-step task execution

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can independently plan, reason, and take actions to accomplish complex goals. Unlike traditional AI models that simply generate responses, Agentic AI can break down tasks, interact with tools, and adapt based on results.

Key Characteristics of Agentic AI

Autonomy

Operates independently with minimal human intervention.

Planning

Breaks complex goals into smaller steps and executes them sequentially.

Tool Usage

Uses APIs, databases, search engines, and software tools.

Adaptation

Adjusts strategies based on feedback and results.

Agentic AI vs Traditional AI

Feature Traditional AI Agentic AI

Decision Making Limited Autonomous planning
Tool Integration Minimal Extensive tool usage
Adaptability Static responses Dynamic and adaptive
